I hope Friday the 13th is'nt a bad omen but today Nagstamon 3.18.1 comes out with some bugfixes:

- fixed truststore support on Windows
- fixed Alertmanager and Prometheus support
- fixed signing of Windows binaries
- fixed Wayland issue

As always get your copy of Nagstamon 3.18.1 at [https://nagstamon.de/download](https://nagstamon.de/download)
